Online Retail Sales Analysis using SQL and Excel

Project Overview

This project analyzes online retail transaction data using SQL (Microsoft Access) and Microsoft Excel. The objective was to clean the dataset, identify sales trends, analyze customer and product performance, and generate actionable business insights.

Dataset

- Online Retail Dataset
- 541,911 transaction records
- Customer, product, invoice, pricing, and country-level information

Tools Used

- Microsoft Access (SQL)
- Microsoft Excel

Data Quality Checks

- Blank Customer IDs
- Blank Product Descriptions
- Negative Quantities
- Zero Unit Prices
- Negative Unit Prices
- Cancelled Invoices

Analysis Performed

1. Total Revenue Analysis
2. Revenue by Country
3. Top Products by Quantity Sold
4. Top Products by Revenue
5. Top Customers by Revenue
6. Monthly Revenue Trend Analysis

Key Insights

- Revenue peaked during November 2011, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- The United Kingdom generated significantly higher revenue than all other countries.
- High-revenue products differed from high-volume products.
- A small number of customers contributed a substantial share of total revenue.
- Revenue increased significantly during the final quarter of the year.

Deliverables

- SQL Queries
- Business Insights Report
- Data Quality Assessment
- Revenue Trend Visualization
- Country and Product Performance Analysis
